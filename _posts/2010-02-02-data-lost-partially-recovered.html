I could have paid the web host $75 to recover the data but being that this is solely for my blog I opted not to do that. Rather I have recovered most (if not all) of my missing posts by using Google Cache. I did this by searching Google for my site and then the /YYYY/MM/ string and clicking the cache link in the search results. I do regret that all of my meta data such as comments/views etc for all of my posts have not been nor can they be recovered. I apologize but at-least the content is there so that when people click web links they are not confronted with a 404.
