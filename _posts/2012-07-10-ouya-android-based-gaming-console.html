I was unable to support the project on Kickstarter but I will buy one if they become a reality. A $99 gaming console will break into the market, I'm sure it's graphics will not be as great as the new consoles which are on the horizon but this will change how consoles are seen. The best part is we anticipated it would be funded by 5pm today and it was in-fact. But the best part is that it went from $925,000 to around $950,353 in just 10 minutes.
